Fintech sector

The combination of Prytek’s capabilities, fintech portfolio and Delta Capita’s Financial Services industry expertise, established one of the leading fintech platforms in Europe providing solutions across global financial markets

Delta Capita

Headquartered in London with offices around the world, Delta Capita Group is a global consulting, solutions and managed service provider. Delta Capita Group work with the world’s top financial institutions to help them comply with regulations, simplify operations, reduce costs and innovate their business models and leverage leading fintechs.

Delta Capita is led by senior industry experts with proven expertise in helping firms innovate and transform their businesses, as well as reduce capital and operating expenses by offering consortia and managed utility service models.

Edutech & HRtech sector

The world workforce is transforming to digital skills. Skilling and education, whether in classes or online, has become the fastest growing segment in many economies. Prytek’s portfolio led by HackerU’s global reach, provides an unparalleled solution to this market demand, offering a wide range of HR and skilling capabilities in the USA, Europe and Asia.

HackerU

HackerU’s mission is to transform learning for people from varying socioeconomic backgrounds and build the world’s cyber and digital workforces by using of technology innovation, industry-leading services, and market-driven trainings.

HackerU offers students and academic institutions continuing education opportunities in digital technology and cybersecurity via bespoke curricula tailored by industry experts to today’s job market. HackerU also provides organizations, governments, and companies with best-in-class managed security services (MSSP).
